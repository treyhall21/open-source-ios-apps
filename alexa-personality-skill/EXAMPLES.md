# Example Interactions with the Personality Changer Skill

> **Note:** Each personality uses SSML voice modulation to change Alexa's tone, pitch, volume, and speaking rate to match the emotional state. The examples below show the text, but when spoken, Alexa's voice will sound notably different for each personality.

## Scenario 1: Starting the Skill and Changing to Happy

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone - medium pitch/volume/pace)*: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Change to happy"
**Alexa** *(excited tone - high pitch, loud volume, cheerful)*: "Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "What's your current mood?"
**Alexa** *(excited tone - high pitch, loud volume, cheerful)*: "I'm currently feeling happy. Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful!"

**User**: "Stop"
**Alexa** *(excited tone - high pitch, loud volume, cheerful)*: "Have a wonderful day! Keep smiling!"

## Scenario 2: Switching Between Personalities

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone)*: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Be mad"
**Alexa** *(angry tone - high pitch, loud volume, fast pace)*: "I'm really angry right now! Don't test my patience! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Change to sad"
**Alexa** *(sad tone - low pitch, soft volume, slow pace)*: "Oh... I'm feeling a bit down and sad right now... You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Be neutral"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone)*: "Hello. I'm in a neutral state. How can I help you? You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Cancel"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone)*: "Goodbye. Have a good day."

## Scenario 3: Using Different Phrases

**User**: "Alexa, ask personality changer to be annoyed"
**Alexa** *(annoyed tone - sarcastic delivery)*: "Ugh, I'm feeling pretty annoyed. What do you want? You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Switch to upset personality"
**Alexa** *(upset tone - low pitch, soft volume, slow pace)*: "I'm quite upset right now. This isn't going well... You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "How are you feeling?"
**Alexa** *(upset tone - low pitch, soft volume, slow pace)*: "I'm currently feeling upset. I'm quite upset right now. This isn't going well..."

**User**: "Stop"
**Alexa** *(upset tone - low pitch, soft volume, slow pace)*: "I need some time alone. Goodbye."

## Scenario 4: Getting Help

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone)*: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Help"
**Alexa** *(neutral tone)*: "This skill allows me to change my personality! You can say things like 'change to happy personality' or 'be sad' or 'what's your current mood'. I can be happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What would you like me to do?"

**User**: "Become cheerful"
**Alexa** *(excited tone - high pitch, loud volume, cheerful)*: "Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

## Voice Characteristics by Personality

Each personality has distinct voice characteristics implemented through SSML:

- **Happy**: High pitch, loud volume, medium pace, excited emotional tone
- **Sad**: Low pitch, soft volume, slow pace
- **Mad**: High pitch, loud volume, fast pace (sounds rushed and irritated)
- **Annoyed**: Medium pitch/volume/pace (sarcastic, obnoxious delivery)
- **Upset**: Low pitch, soft volume, slow pace (troubled tone)
- **Neutral**: Medium pitch/volume/pace (professional, calm)

## Available Voice Commands

### Changing Personality
- "Change to [personality]"
- "Be [personality]"
- "Change your personality to [personality]"
- "Become [personality]"
- "Change mood to [personality]"
- "Switch to [personality] personality"
- "I want you to be [personality]"
- "Act [personality]"
- "Set personality to [personality]"

### Checking Current Personality
- "What's your personality"
- "What's your current personality"
- "What's your mood"
- "What mood are you in"
- "How are you feeling"
- "Tell me your personality"

### Available Personalities
1. **Happy** (synonyms: cheerful, joyful)
2. **Sad** (synonyms: depressed, down)
3. **Mad** (synonyms: angry, furious)
4. **Annoyed**
5. **Upset**
6. **Neutral**
