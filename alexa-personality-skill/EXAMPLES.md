# Example Interactions with the Personality Changer Skill

## Scenario 1: Starting the Skill and Changing to Happy

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa**: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Change to happy"
**Alexa**: "Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "What's your current mood?"
**Alexa**: "I'm currently feeling happy. Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful!"

**User**: "Stop"
**Alexa**: "Have a wonderful day! Keep smiling!"

---

## Scenario 2: Switching Between Personalities

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa**: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Be mad"
**Alexa**: "I'm really angry right now! Don't test my patience! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Change to sad"
**Alexa**: "Oh... I'm feeling a bit down and sad right now... You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Be neutral"
**Alexa**: "Hello. I'm in a neutral state. How can I help you? You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Cancel"
**Alexa**: "Goodbye. Have a good day."

---

## Scenario 3: Using Different Phrases

**User**: "Alexa, ask personality changer to be annoyed"
**Alexa**: "Ugh, I'm feeling pretty annoyed. What do you want? You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "Switch to upset personality"
**Alexa**: "I'm quite upset right now. This isn't going well... You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

**User**: "How are you feeling?"
**Alexa**: "I'm currently feeling upset. I'm quite upset right now. This isn't going well..."

**User**: "Stop"
**Alexa**: "I need some time alone. Goodbye."

## Scenario 4: Getting Help

**User**: "Alexa, open personality changer"
**Alexa**: "Welcome to the Personality Changer skill! I'm currently feeling neutral. You can ask me to change my personality to happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What personality would you like me to have?"

**User**: "Help"
**Alexa**: "This skill allows me to change my personality! You can say things like 'change to happy personality' or 'be sad' or 'what's your current mood'. I can be happy, sad, mad, annoyed, upset, or neutral. What would you like me to do?"

**User**: "Become cheerful"
**Alexa**: "Yay! I'm feeling so happy and cheerful! Everything is wonderful! You can ask me to do something or change my personality again."

## Available Voice Commands

### Changing Personality
- "Change to [personality]"
- "Be [personality]"
- "Change your personality to [personality]"
- "Become [personality]"
- "Change mood to [personality]"
- "Switch to [personality] personality"
- "I want you to be [personality]"
- "Act [personality]"
- "Set personality to [personality]"

### Checking Current Personality
- "What's your personality"
- "What's your current personality"
- "What's your mood"
- "What mood are you in"
- "How are you feeling"
- "Tell me your personality"

### Available Personalities
1. **Happy** (synonyms: cheerful, joyful)
2. **Sad** (synonyms: depressed, down)
3. **Mad** (synonyms: angry, furious)
4. **Annoyed**
5. **Upset**
6. **Neutral**
